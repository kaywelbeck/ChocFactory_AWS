About Us

We are a group of enthusiastic Netcom students who have been learning about Amazon Web Services (AWS). With a passion for technology and innovation, we decided to embark on our first project: creating an e-commerce website dedicated to selling delicious chocolate!

Our goal is to provide a delightful online shopping experience while showcasing our skills and knowledge in cloud computing and web development. We are excited to deploy our site on AWS, utilizing its powerful services to ensure scalability, security, and performance.
